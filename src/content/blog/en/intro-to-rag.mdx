## What is RAG?

Retrieval-Augmented Generation combines the power of large language models with external knowledge retrieval...

## The Architecture

A typical RAG pipeline consists of three stages: indexing, retrieval, and generation...

### Indexing

Documents are chunked, embedded, and stored in a vector database...

```typescript
const chunks = splitDocument(document, { chunkSize: 512, overlap: 50 });
const embeddings = await embed(chunks);
await vectorStore.upsert(embeddings);
```

## When to Use RAG

RAG is ideal when your LLM needs access to domain-specific or frequently updated knowledge.
